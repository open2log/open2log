Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Open2Log ("we", "our", "us") is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping consumers compare grocery prices. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (required for registration)
  • Hashed password (we never store your password in plain text)
  • Bank reference number (for NGO membership payments)

Usage Data

  • Products you scan and prices you submit
  • Shops you visit (only when you confirm your location)
  • Shopping lists (only if you use the shared shopping list feature)

Location Data

  • Your location is only accessed when you use the "nearby shops" feature
  • We ask for confirmation before associating you with a specific shop
  • Precise location is never stored on our servers

Images

  • Product barcode images (cropped to barcode area only)
  • Price label images
  • Product images (only if you choose to contribute)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our price comparison service
  • To verify and validate price submissions
  • To build a comprehensive product database
  • To enable shared shopping lists among members
  • For research and statistical analysis (anonymized)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share:

  • Anonymized price data for public research
  • Aggregated statistics (no individual identification)
  • Shopping lists with users you explicitly share them with

5. Data Storage

  • Account data is stored on servers in the European Union (Finland)
  • Images are stored in Cloudflare R2 (with EU data residency option)
  • Shared shopping lists are stored in Cloudflare D1 (edge database)
  • Local data on your device is stored in encrypted local storage

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Finnish data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Price submissions: Indefinitely (for historical price tracking)
  • Images: 1 year after submission (then deleted)
  • Shopping lists: Until you delete them

8. Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt
  • Server access is restricted and audited
  • Regular security reviews are conducted

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries:

  • Email: privacy@open2log.com
  • Data Controller: Open2Log ry, Finland

12. NGO Membership

If you become an NGO member:

  • Your bank reference is used to verify membership payments
  • Payment information is handled by your bank, not us
  • Member status is stored to provide access to member-only features
